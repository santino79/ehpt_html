Elizabeth Hylands is a certified Personal Trainer and Fitness Instructor based in Banbridge, County Down, Northern Ireland.

From her own private fitness studio at Cura Fitness, she provides personal training to women who want to make a real positive difference to their lives.

Whether you want to lose body fat and reshape your figure or increase your muscle mass and strength, Elizabeth has the experience and proven strategies to help you achieve your personal training goals.

Why Do Personal Training?

Improve Confidence

Reduce your negative self-talk and focus on your personal positives as you feel your body and mind change for the better.

Build Strength

Building strong muscles helps build stronger bones, ligaments and tendons, reduces injury risk and makes you feel better mentally as well as physically.

Accountability

Setting realistic goals and getting regular encouragement to help you achieve them over the long term is one of the best reasons to work with a personal trainer.
